The proceedings contain 40 papers. The topics discussed include: acoustic emission signal processing of the pressure relief valve; program system for signal processing in innovative education and research; automatic measurement of the antenna directional characteristics; the possibilities of utilization of mobile pulsed radar sensors; supercapacitor for engine cranking; new subject 'energy harvesting' for education of mechatronics; affect of operation conditions of triple half bridge series resonant inverter for harmonic distortion and power factor; method cooperation in optimization process; determination of air permeability of a linen material; design of algorithm of inverse viewing transformation with using multistage correlation analysis of stereoimage; magnetic field in aerial toroidal and poloidal coil system arrangement; image generator framework; and a data integrity in distributed .net applications.
