The proceedings contain 71 papers. The topics discussed include: construction and practical application peculiarities of systems for power facilities remote monitoring and diagnostics; the optimization of energy parameters of the electrical systems of charge of supercapacitor from accumulator battery; features of an e-learning software for teaching and self-studying of electrical engineering; the influence of parameters of evolutionary selection of model form on its precision and complexity; anisotropic properties of electrical steels; macromodel of dynamics of the electric power generated by small hydroelectric power station based on interval data analysis; comparison of application features of static and differential parameters; electronic current transformer gapped sensor design parameters calculation and selection; and control of transistors in AC voltage PWM controller with elimination of commutation overvoltages.
