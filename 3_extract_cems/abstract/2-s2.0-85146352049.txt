The proceedings contain 25 papers. The topics discussed include: object recognition for a humanoid robot based on a microcontroller; approximation of supercapacitor charging process using fractional order transfer functions; electrochemical reactor with an ultrasonic piezoelectric ceramic transducer for manufacturing nanoporous aluminum oxide membrane; features of the construction of a robotic mobile platform for movement on orthogonal routes; ferrofluid droplet robot manipulation using rule-based control strategy; finite element analysis of magnetic shielding for a miniaturized mems displacement sensor; functional testing of electrically conductive sphere valve under short-term induction heating; improving accuracy in information systems based on mems technology; investigation of the heating of the antenna-feeder element during the propagation of an amplitude-modulated radio impulse; and magnetic shielding of linear variable differential transformer with pure iron foils.
