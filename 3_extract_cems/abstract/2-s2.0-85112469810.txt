The proceedings contain 11 papers. The topics discussed include: high-frequency validation of predictions for energy production from large solar PV plants; techno-economic analysis of a solar tower CSP Plant with thermal energy storage in southern Honduras; research on the hydrogen production technology; techno-financial comparison between photovoltaic systems with bifacial and monofacial technology in the location of Naco and Marcovia, Honduras; rapid hydropower potential assessment for remote area by using global data; influence of stairwell ventilation state on fire behavior and smoke temperature distribution in a full-scale high-rise building; characters of different materials as supercapacitor electrodes; and the influence of hydrothermal effect on migration of contaminants in porous media.
