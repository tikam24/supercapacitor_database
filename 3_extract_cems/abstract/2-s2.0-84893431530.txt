The proceedings contain 17 papers. The topics discussed include: evaluation of the through-the-road architecture for plug-in hybrid electric vehicle powertrains; development of an educational small scale hybrid electric vehicle (HEV) setup; economic analysis of electric vehicle supply equipment - a utility's perspective; modeling the closed-loop interaction between the grid and a multifunctional electric vehicle charging station integrated with an active power filter; reduction of PV reverse power flow through the usage of EV's battery with consideration of the demand and solar radiation forecast; model predictive control for the power-split between supercapacitor and battery for automotive applications; optimization of the integration of semiconductors in vehicles; RACE: a centralized platform computer based architecture for automotive applications; and key challenges within the definition of a regulatory framework for electric vehicles: experience from Colombia.
