The proceedings contain 103 papers. The topics discussed include: study on noise characteristics of 500kV oil-immersed transformer under no-load voltage condition; energy measurement scheme of off-board charger suitable for virtual load verification; research on regenerative braking energy recovery strategy of electric vehicle; optimization and feasibility analysis of pico-hydro generation system used in small agricultural pipe network; stability improvement of electric ship propulsion system using supercapacitor; research on liquid cooling technology in the field of electric bicycle charging; dynamic analysis of a closed brayton cycle using supercritical carbon dioxide; an integrated prediction and optimization method for drilling rate of penetration; and hydrodynamic analysis of photovoltaic power generation aquaculture platform under wave action.
