The proceedings contain 349 papers and 5 PowerPoint presentations. The topics discussed include: the cell versus the system: standardization challenges for electricity storage devices; supercapacitor enhanced electric power systems for personnel transport systems; the hybrid vehicle market 2008-2015 impact on the battery business; development of an electrically rechargeable zinc air battery for electric vehicles; key technologies used in Olympic electric bus; life cycle cost analysis of alternative vehicles and fuels in Belgium; evaluation of control system for mode change clutch of HEV by using embedded system; low-pass filtered power-flow control in series hybrid electric vehicle; an optimization approach to hybrid electric vehicle preliminary design; an optimization approach to hybrid electric vehicle preliminary design; and realization and implementation of adaptive control for permanent magnet synchronous motor on an electric vehicle.
