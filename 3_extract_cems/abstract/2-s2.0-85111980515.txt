The proceedings contain 15 papers. The topics discussed include: construction of forecast model for power demand and PV power generation using tensor product spline function; analytical output of smart piezoelectric cantilever beam; safety assessment of electromagnetic exposure of superconducting magnetic levitation track; optimal design of impulse turbine prototype of oscillating water column type wave energy power generation device; effect of activation methods on the supercapacitor performance of hydrochar derived from oil palm empty fruit bunch; a study on an impact of tower surge impedance against lightning surge phenomena; cost-benefit analysis of the implementation of off-grid photovoltaic systems in the northwest residential sector of San Pedro Sula, Honduras; and adaptive thermal comfort and energy saving potential in naturally ventilated school building in Nepal.
