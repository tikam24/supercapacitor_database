The proceedings contain 64 papers. The topics discussed include: an I2V communication network for driver assistance in public transport; performance assessment of an automotive-grade TO-247 IGBT copacked with SiC diode in a bidirectional buck converter; high efficiency bidirectional SiC-based power converter for V2G/V2H applications in a nano/microgrid scenario; design of a high-speed electric propulsion system for electric vehicles; electrothermal battery pack model for automotive application: design and validation; supercapacitor assisted hybrid electric vehicle powertrain and power selection using fuzzy rule-based algorithm; a holistic approach to improve a liquid cooled battery module; and study of behavior of P-gate in power GaN under positive voltage.
