The proceedings contain 328 papers. The topics discussed include: a cable-tie-type parylene cuff electrode for peripheral nerve interfaces; whole-angle-mode micromachined fused-silica birdbath resonator gyroscope (WA-BRG); invar-36 micro hemispherical shell resonators; liquid-filled sealed MEMS capsules fabricated by fluidic self-assembly; bionic skins using flexible organic devices; encased cantilevers for low-noise force and mass sensing in liquids; a vertically integrated nanoscale tipped microprobe intracellular electrode array; ALD ruthenium oxide-carbon nanotube electrodes for supercapacitor applications; fabrication method to a high resolution control in the space of cell culturing environment with microfluidic system; and electromagnetic energy harvester with an in-phase vibration bandwidth broadening technique.
