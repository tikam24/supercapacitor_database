Invited for this month's cover picture is the group of Prof. Raz Jelinek at Ben-Gurion University. The cover picture shows the incorporation of Ni in the RuO2-based supercapacitor through a simple electrochemical process. Nickel incorporation affected both the morphology and electrochemical performance, particularly enabling operation of the supercapacitor in high current densities and high frequencies.
