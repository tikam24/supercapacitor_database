The proceedings contain 10 papers. The topics discussed include: detection and prevention of hypervisor and VM attacks; android application development based tourist guiding mobile application using Java & XML coding; performance and stability analysis of dye-sensitized solar cell; a facile method for synthesizing graphene polyaniline composite for supercapacitor electrode fabrication and its enhanced performance evaluation; breakthroughs of using photodynamic therapy and gold nanoparticles in cancer treatment; neuromorphic high dimensional computing architecture for classification applications; simulation of thin-film transistor based winner-take-all circuit; and performance investigation of nanoscaled GaN-BTG MOSFET for analog/linearity and low power applications.
