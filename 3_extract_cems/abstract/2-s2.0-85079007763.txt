The proceedings contain 67 papers. The topics discussed include: analysis of battery voltage and stability using gaussian probability distribution theory; production of biodiesel from rubber seed oil using heterogeneous acid catalyst produce from rice washing wastewater; the performance of symmetrical and asymmetrical supercapacitor of aquatic biomass carbon nanosheet; effect of amorphous rice husk silica addition on the structure of asphalt composite; design multi field electromagnet based on copper plate with single electric current; modeling of spin-polarized currents in zinc blende-based magnetic tunnel junctions featuring the dresselhaus effect; cooling times dependence on the glassy niti at extremely low temperature: a result from rapid solidification using molecular dynamics simulations; and synthesis graphite polystyrene composite material and its application.
