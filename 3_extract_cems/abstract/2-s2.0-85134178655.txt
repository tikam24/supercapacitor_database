The proceedings contain 21 papers. The special focus in this conference is on Corrosion Prevention Symposium for Research Scholars. The topics include: Effect of Hydrogen Sulfide and Chloride on the Passive Film Structures of Stainless Steel Type 316L; The Influence of Sulphate-Reducing Bacteria on Surface Characterisation of Carbon Steel in CO2 Environment; Corrosion Under Insulation (CUI) Monitoring: Improvise a Smart Way to Detect CUI; Electrochemical Studies of Date Palm Seed as a Green Corrosion Inhibitor for Carbon Steel in 0.5 M H2SO4 and CH3COOH Conditions; electrochemical Monitoring of Oxygen Potential on Fe-Cr Alloy Surface During High Temperature Oxidation in Dry and Humid Conditions; effect of Water Resistivity and Coating Material for Cathodic Protection System to Achieve Cathodic Protection Criteria; effectiveness of Sodium Dodecylbenzene Sulfonate and Zinc Sulfate as Corrosion Inhibitors for Mild Steel in Sodium Chloride Solution; high-Temperature Corrosion of Hot-Dipped Aluminizing on Carbon Steel in Dry and Wet Environments; Effect of Graphite Particles on Corrosion Resistance Behaviour of Palm Oil-Based UV-Curable Coating; corrosion Behavior of Aluminum Alloys in Hydrogen Chloride Containing Environments; mechanical and Corrosion Properties Investigation of Sn-9Zn Solder with the Additions of Nickel-Coated Precipitated Calcium Carbonate; effect of Volatile Corrosion Inhibitor Dosage Towards Top and Bottom of the Line Corrosion; preface; high Temperature Oxidation of T91 Alloy of Steam Reformer; producing Blast Furnace Slag Cement Clinker by Utilizing Redox Reaction Approach; Top of Line Corrosion (TLC) Behaviour with Presence of Gas Condensate in CO2 Wet Gas Pipeline; electrochemical Characterization of Cleaning Nickel Foam Current Collector for Supercapacitor Application.
