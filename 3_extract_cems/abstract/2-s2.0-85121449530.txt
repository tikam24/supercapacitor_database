The proceedings contain 32 papers. The topics discussed include: identification of crustal thickness in central part of Sumatra using teleseismic receiver function method; crustal structure study in lampung region using teleseismic receiver function analysis; comparison study of crustal structure in aceh region based on volcanic arc system using receiver function method; geothermal reservoir identification in Way Ratai area based on gravity data analysis; getting to know the last five years trend on microcontroller research: a bibliometric analysis; determination of resonance parameters of the PANI thin film fabricated using spin coating method; study of electrical and thermal properties of PVA-nanosilica as a candidate for supercapacitor separators; and effect of adding of green betel leaf extract and honey on mechanical properties of chitosan/PVA hydrogel wound plaster.
