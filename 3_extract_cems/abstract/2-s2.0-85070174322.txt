The proceedings contains 47 papers from the 2001 Journal of Power Sources. Topics discussed include: standby batteries; lithium ion batteries; polymer electrolytes; lithium batteries; interfacial resistance in lithium cells; power sources for implantable cardioverter defibrillators; true prismatic lithium-ion cells; compact high power sources; solid electrolytes; power limitations at porous supercapacitor electrodes; and nickel metal hydride batteries.
