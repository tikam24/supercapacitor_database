The proceedings contain 195 papers. The topics discussed include: numerical study of heat transfer enhancement in fin and tube heat exchangers using curved semi wavy vortex generators; biosynthesis and characterization of silver nanoparticles from penicillium notatum and their application to improve efficiency of antibiotics; influence of SiC powders on aluminum alloy 7075-T6 joints by friction stir welding process; analysis of the barriers in implementing green supply chain management (GSCM) practices: a hybrid approach; effect of mixed ions and ion irradiation on ionic conductivity of solid polymer electrolytes; carbon quantum dot-polypyrrole nanocomposite for supercapacitor electrodes; investigation of mechanical properties of glass fiber reinforced honeycomb panels; and experimental investigations on rectangular dielectric resonator loaded on a microstrip line.
