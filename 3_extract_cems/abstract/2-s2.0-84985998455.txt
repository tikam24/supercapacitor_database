The proceedings contain 98 papers. The topics discussed include: an adaline based capacitor voltage estimation algorithm for modular multilevel converters; a high-efficiency bidirectional non-isolated multi-input converter; safety system for handling medium and high voltage apparatus; risk based asset management of electrical distribution network; methodology for evaluation effectiveness and efficiency in management of IT-projects; converter with galvanic isolation for supercapacitor charging; prediction of the parameters of the thermoelectric cooling systems based on peltier elements with neural network; and nearest fixed clustering algorithm (NFCA) for wireless sensor networks.
