in this paper, a kind of intelligent wireless charging device is studied by taking the intelligent car as an example, the system is mainly composed of MSP430 control circuit, DC motor, wireless charging and discharging module, voltage stabilizing application circuit and super capacitor energy storage module, Through the MSP430 single-chip microcomputer control car operation, set the car charging time, control the wireless charging coil module, the use of super-capacitor charging, DC-DC conversion circuit for car charging, after repeated testing,The experimental results show that the device has high charging efficiency and low power consumption, and can charge the car at a set time.
