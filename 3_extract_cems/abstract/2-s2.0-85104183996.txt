The proceedings contain 121 papers. The topics discussed include: research on oxygen saturation based on statistical analyses; application of machine learning in recommendation system; research on location of cold chain logistics distribution center with low carbon in Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei area on the basis of RNA-artificial fish swarm algorithm; health evaluation method of supercapacitor based on data mining; research on network traffic anomaly detection method based on deep learning; microservice monitoring and handoff algorithm based on dual-node hot standby; application of particle swarm optimization on reliability sampling inspection program for success or failure product; and quantitative research on the science and technology investment and economic growth of Guangdong province from the perspective of intelligent data analysis.
