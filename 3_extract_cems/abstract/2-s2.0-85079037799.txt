The proceedings contain 648 papers. The topics discussed include: bilevel arbitrage potential evaluation for grid-scale energy storage considering wind power and LMP smoothing effect; distributed robust energy management of a multi-microgrid system in the real-time energy market; fast feeder PV hosting capacity using swarm based intelligent distribution node selection; prediction of supercapacitor discharge time using Peukerts Law; power flow solution methods for combined transmission and distribution systems; a simplified synchronous machine model for virtual synchronous generator implementation; quantifying electric load flexibility using smart meter data; and impact of increasing the number of type-3 wind turbines on stability of power-system dynamics.
