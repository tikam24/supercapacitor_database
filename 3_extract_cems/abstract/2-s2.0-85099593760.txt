The proceedings contain 82 papers. The topics discussed include: kinetic analysis of the vls growth of semiconducting nanowires; wearable e-textile wireless RF power supply based on a textile supercapacitor and a flexible rectenna filament; carbon nanotube/PEDOT: PSS composite-based flexible temperature sensor with enhanced response and recovery time; embroidered textile capacitive sensor for sucrose solutions measurement; flexible and wearable ultrasonic sensors and method for classifying individual finger flexions; variation-tolerant digital circuit design for printed/flexible electronics; investigation of temperature effect on the porosity of a fabric based porous capacitive pressure sensor; and development of a PPG sensor array as a wearable device to monitor cardiovascular metrics.
