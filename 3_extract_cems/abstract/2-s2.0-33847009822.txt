The proceedings contain 38 papers. The topics discussed include: photovoltaic system connected to the load and the utility with unity power factor; the laboratory model of wind power generator; controlled and decoupled distributed generation; power quality in high-tech plants: A case study; grid voltage sags effects on frequency converter drives and controlled rectifier drives; sensorless control of permanent magnet synchronous motor using matrix converters; direct torque control for induction motors using matrix converters; analysis of permanent magnet multipole synchronous generators for wind applications; supercapacitor storage application for reduction drive negative impact on supply grid; fault detection techniques for induction motors; active power filters synchronisation in networks with large frequency deviation; a new low-distorting three-phase diode rectifier employing optimum ripple power control; and synchronized active power filter.
