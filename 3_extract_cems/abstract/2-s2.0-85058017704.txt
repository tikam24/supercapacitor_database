The development of crystal types, preparation methods and applications for MnOOH was introduced. Preparation methods for MnOOH including hydrothermal method, liquid precipitation method, reflux method and thermal decomposition method, and its applications in supercapacitor, lithium ion and sodium ion batteries, catalysis, adsorption and separation, and its applications used as precursor are mainly introduced. Moreover, the development trends of MnOOH are pointed out.
