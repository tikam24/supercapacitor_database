The proceedings contain 69 papers. The topics discussed include: power transformer mechanical condition assessment with a vibration-based diagnostic method; research and simulation of overhead power line uprating using advanced conductors; analysis of cyclic DC load currents for intelligent electrical protection; development of single-loop current sensorless control for bidirectional three-phase PWM rectifier; considerations for phasor measurement unit introduction in distribution system; current regulation of AC machines under voltage constraints; system for start of internal combustion engine with hybrid battery-supercapacitor source; context application to improve LED lighting control systems; optimization of concentrated solar thermoelectric generator system for highest yearly electric output; and implementation of fast and accurate modeling method of electric drives.
