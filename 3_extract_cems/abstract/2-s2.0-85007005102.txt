The patent data of supercapacitor from the Derwent Innovations Index (DII) database were used to study the technical competitive situation of the supercapacitor industry through the analysis of competitive environments and major competitors from the perspective of competitive intelligence. By calculating the indicators of the patent technology such as life cycle, R&D priorities, distribution of major patent priority countries, major research institutions and others, the technological position of China in the field was clearly determined, and the strategies and suggestions for the development of China's supercapacitor industry were presented.
