Magnetic nanoparticles with their unique properties become an alarming topic by various researchers. These nanoparticles have a high surface area, superparamagnetic, biocompatible, and easy to synthesize. These properties made the nanoparticles capable to apply in various applications such as wastewater treatment, biomedical application, supercapacitor, etc. The nanoparticles are used as the photocatalyst and adsorbent of various dyes. They are also used in the delivery of drugs to the cancerous cells and help in the treatment of cancer in the hyperthermia process. Magnetic nanoparticles have high specific capacitance and have the potential in making supercapacitors.
