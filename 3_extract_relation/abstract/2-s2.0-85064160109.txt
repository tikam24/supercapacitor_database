NiCo 2 O 4 electrodes prepared with different reagents by a facile hydrothermal method and characterized by XRD, XPS, SEM and TEM analysis. The urchin flower like nanostructure of NiCo 2 O 4 electrode is prepared with hexamethylenetetramine and urea as reagents which exhibit excellent electrochemical performance, a high specific capacitance of 1839.26 Fg −1 at current density of 0.9 Ag −1 , with high energy density of 40.13 Wh kg −1 at the power density of 240 W kg −1 and capacitance retention of 87.13% after 2500 cycles.
