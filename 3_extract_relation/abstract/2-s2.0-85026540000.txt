The last decade has witnessed fast developments and substantial achievements that have been shaping the field of stretchable electronics. This chapter reviews state-of-theart development in the field of ionic liquid for supercapacitors and discusses their specific features. The main themes discussed are based on ionic liquids as novel electrolytes for supercapacitors. This is followed by information on Ionic liquid as High Performance Electrolytes for Supercapacitors and current research activities and finally a conclusion.
