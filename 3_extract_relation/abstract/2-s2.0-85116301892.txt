The proceedings contain 61 papers. The topics discussed include: generative skeletal joint-based autoencoder for 3d human point clouds reconstruction; a yellowness index simulation model; two-phase switching optimization strategy in LSTM model for predictive maintenance; LPG gas usage and leakage detection using IoT in Brunei; energy management system using cascade fuzzy for hybrid battery and supercapacitor in electric vehicles; GPU-accelerated state estimation for large-scale power systems; envisioning 6g molecular communication for IoBNT diagnostic systems; PV solar technology for energy cost and emissions reduction in rural area; GPU-accelerated power system sensitivity analysis; and service quality analysis of internet access from universal service obligations fund in Indonesia.
