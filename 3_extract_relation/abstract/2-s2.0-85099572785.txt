The proceedings contain 17 papers. The topics discussed include: a state observer from multilevel quantized outputs; review of NILM applications in smart grids: power quality assessment and assisted independent living; a technical and economic approach to multilevel optimization models for electricity demand considering user-supplier interaction; control of a centrifugal pump as a turbine in an energy recovery system; energy management via passivity-based control for battery/supercapacitor electric vehicles; a collection of definitions and fundamentals for a design-oriented inductor model; model predictive periodic output path following; nonlinear fault-tolerant model predictive control strategy for industrial processes; and economic model predictive control for energy management in a microgrid.
