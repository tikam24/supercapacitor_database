The proceedings contain 57 papers. The topics discussed include: computational modelling of freezing of water droplet impacting on an ice surface; enhancement of electrochemical properties of carbon solution doped bismuth ferrite for supercapacitor application; optimization of performance and emission characteristics of a diesel engine fueled with karanja biodiesel using Grey-Taguchi method; finite element analysis of a single coil straight distal tip microcatheter; fabrication of radiation crosslinked and MWCNT reinforced ethylene acrylic elastomer nanocomposites: evaluation of mechanical, dynamic mechanical, thermal and dielectric properties; single-walled carbon nanotube filled thermoplastic polyurethane nanocomposites: influence of ionic liquid on dielectric properties; and finite element analysis for predicting the vibration characteristics of natural fiber reinforced epoxy composites.
