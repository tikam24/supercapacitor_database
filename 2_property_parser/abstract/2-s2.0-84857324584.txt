The proceedings contain 31 papers. The topics discussed include: SiC power devices for next generation energy efficiency; reliability-driven SiC power device development for army applications; supercapacitor/battery hybrids for energy harvesting applications; iii-nitride heterojunction field-effect transistors and heterojunction bipolar transistors for next-generation power electronics; high voltage normally-off transistors and efficient schottky diodes based on GaN technology; merits of buried grid technology for advanced SIC device concepts; modeling and simulation of silicon carbide power systems; packaging and modeling of SiC power modules; silicon carbide bipolar power devices; degradation of SiC bipolar devices: a review of likely causes and recent advances in its understanding; mitigating defects within silicon carbide epitaxy; and numerical simulation of the GaN growth process in a MOCVD process.
