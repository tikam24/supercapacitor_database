The proceedings contain 336 papers. The topics discussed include: enhancement of dynamic stability of power systems using a converter with SMES; odometry-free mobile robot localization using bearing only beacons; more-electric aircraft electrical power system accelerated functional modeling; concept, design and coupled electro-thermal analysis of new hybrid drive vehicle for public transport; ac drive with permanent magnet synchronous motor and sensorless control; evaluation of motor PWM loss increase due to zero common-mode voltage modulation strategy of multilevel converter; new bi-directional dc/dc converter for supercapacitor interfacing in high-power applications; application protocols for safety-critical can-networked systems; features of sinusoidal and resistive current loads in harmonic distorted networks; analysis of permissible state of flying capacitors multilevel inverter switch; and distributed control computer backbone communication channel of electric locomotive with effective DMA support.
