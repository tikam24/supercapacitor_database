The proceedings contain 131 papers. The topics discussed include: direct electric in-wheel driving of a bicycle using reluctant motors; comparison of three different electric powertrains for the use in high performance electric go-kart; a control algorithm of AC-brushless driven for railways traction application; a novel on-line state-of-charge estimation algorithm for valve-regulated lead-acid batteries used in hybrid electric vehicles; a novel ultra compact surface mountable 1A/500V rated intelligent power module (IPM) utilizing silicon on insulator (SOI) technology; energy-retaining snubbers for an AC arc welding machine; a robust control of dynamic voltage restorer using fuzzy logic; minimum supercapacitor choice to provide current step variation in FC PEM; and state variables analysis of passive filters.
