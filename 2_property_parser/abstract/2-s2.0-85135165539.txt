The proceedings contain 51 papers. The topics discussed include: growth and characterizations of nonlinear optical material: sodium magnesium tetrachloride (SMGTC) single crystal; synthesis, optical, vibrational spectroscopy, experimental and theoretical investigation of nonlinear optical properties of L-argininium hydrogen squarate; structure-activity relationship of quercetin and its tumor necrosis factor alpha inhibition activity by computational and machine learning methods; growth, theoretical, optical and dielectric properties of a semi-organic zinc acetate picolinium succinate (ZAPS) single crystals for nonlinear optical applications; synthesis, growth, vibrational analysis, hardness studies and theoretical calculations on inorganic NLO sulphamic acid lithium chloride single crystal; and synthesis and characterization of Zn doped CdS nanoparticles as electrode material for supercapacitor application.
