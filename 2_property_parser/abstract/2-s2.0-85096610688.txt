The proceedings contain 729 papers. The topics discussed include: flow behavior of a model colloid-polymer mixture using mode-coupling theory; impact of synthesis technique on dysprosium doped CuO for supercapacitor applications; nanostructuring at elevated temperatures on ion irradiated CoSi binary mixture; fractal assessment of thin films deposited by random and ballistic deposition models; optical dispersive constants of planarheterojunction TAPC/C70 bilayer; calculation of flux for superconducting wavelength shifter based high energy x-ray diffraction beamline BL-15; electrochemical sensing of dopamine using graphene oxide derived from pine needle bio-waste; and microfluidic synthesis of polystyrene nanoparticles.
