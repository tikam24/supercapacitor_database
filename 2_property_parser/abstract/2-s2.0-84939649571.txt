The proceedings contain 10 papers. The topics discussed include: synchrotron-enabled ex-situ and in-situ mechanistic interrogation of energy storage systems; comparative study of the power performance for advanced Li-ion capacitors with various carbon anodes; indium tin oxide photonic crystal for controllable light coupling in solar cells by an inexpensive soft lithography with HD-DVD and Blu-ray; nanoporous anodic bismuth oxide for electrochemical energy storage; effect of temperature on characteristic of carbon nanotube nano-composite electrode supercapacitor; synthetic strategies impacting voltage, capacity, and current capability of energy storage materials; improvements in Li air batteries through electrocatalysts, electrode structure design and modeling; and dual-function coaxial supercapacitor cable.
