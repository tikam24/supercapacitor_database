The proceedings contain 33 papers. The topics discussed include: entrepreneurial intensions research of business students in the Bulgarian context; reliability estimation using mission profile of high frequency power supply for soldering carbide tips; studying supercapacitor reliability according to operating modes and environmental conditions; energy harvesting measurement of sonic crystal acoustic barrier; preparation and application of composite material as sorbent for oil spills; preparation and characterization of activated carbon derived from lignocellulosic waste biomass; network of autonomous energy storage modules, such as concentrators for charging stations for electric vehicles and smart sities, based on battery systems and a model for selecting the capacity of batteries; and obtaining of activated carbon adsorbents based on wood waste material via chemical activation.
