Ultracapacitors or Supercapacitors have been in the spotlight in recent years, for their advantageous properties like good cyclic stability, high power density and fast chargeâ€“discharge rates. Till date, a wide number of researchers have highlighted the electrode materials, electrolytes and separators to accomplish an economic, efficient system with a focus on high performance. In order to satisfy the increasing demand for energy storage systems, new types of materials are introduced in recent years. Ceramic material stands promising due to its wide-ranged working temperature, high cyclability, excellent electrical conductivity, electron transfer proficiency and so on. As the title implies, the present work focuses on advancements and achievements in ceramic material for supercapacitors along with their fabrication techniques. In addition, the technical viewpoints and challenges faced in the present era for ceramics in supercapacitor applications are discussed.
