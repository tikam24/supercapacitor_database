The proceedings contain 206 papers. The topics discussed include: analysis of research situation and development process in the field of global marine internet of things; research on factors of marine ranch leisure fisheries development based on multiple orderly logit model; optimizing the loaded and empty car flows of railway network with dual objectives; digital prediction modeling of fabric softness based on thickness classification; application of recycling packaging in low carbon supply chain; total nitrogen prediction based on quantum weighted minimal gated unit neural networks in wastewater treatment process; numerical simulation study on the disturbance effect of cable tunnel induced by subway tunnel construction; design and application of integrated circuit virtual simulation experimental system under VR technology; research on non-contact detection method for conductivity of supercapacitor; and optimization of differential load dynamic characteristics of radar antenna folding system based on co-simulation.
