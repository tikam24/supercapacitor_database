The proceedings contain 8 papers. The topics discussed include: D-glucose derived micro/mesoporous carbons for ultra-high rate supercapacitor application; hierarchical nanostructured carbon materials for ultrahigh electrochemical energy storage; effect of pore size distribution on carbon/carbon supercapacitors operating in phosphododecatungstic acid; electrochemical performance of supercapacitors formed by PAni/CF and PAni/CNT/CF; electrochemical storage in Zr-W mixed oxide nanostructured arrays; outer and inner surface contribution of manganese dioxides energy storage characterization by cavity microelectrode technique; synthesis and characterization of vanadium oxide nanotube electrode material for electrochemical capacitors; and fluoroethylene carbonate and propylene carbonate mixtures based electrolytes for supercapacitors.
