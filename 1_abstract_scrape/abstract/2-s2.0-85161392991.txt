The proceedings contain 9 papers. The special focus in this conference is on Research, Development, and Innovation in Renewable Energies. The topics include: Evaluation of the Feasibility of Biogas Production by Anaerobic Digestion from Agro-Industrial Wastes in Los Altos, Jalisco; supercapacitor Based Energy Storage System to Improve Integration of Variable Renewable Energies in Electric Grids; analysis of the Annual Energy Production of the Villonaco Wind Farm; LULC Evolution of Southeast Mexico Based on Geographical Information Systems and Remote Sensing; shallow Geothermal Energy Resources and Thermal Impacts in Buenos Aires City, Argentina; optimal Site Selection for a Mini Hydro-Power Run of River Scheme Using Geographic Information Systems, Considering Civil Work Costs; evaluation of the Solar Organic Rankine Cycle with Different Working Fluids.
