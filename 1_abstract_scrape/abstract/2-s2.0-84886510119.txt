A new Fe3O4-SWCNH nanocomposite was synthesized as an electrode material for supercapacitors. The nanocomposite exhibited a high specific capacitance of 377 F g-1 and delivered a stable discharge capacitance at a current density of 1 A g-1 over 1000 cycles between 0 and 1.2 V.
