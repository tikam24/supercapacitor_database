The proceedings contain 7 papers. The topics discussed include: cathodic electrodeposition of manganese oxide for electrochemical supercapacitors; investigation of cycling stability of Mn-containing oxide supercapacitors; the magnetohydrodynamic effect in electrochemical systems including batteries; cellulose based activated carbon for electrochemical double layer capacitors; characterization of activated nanoporous carbon as double layer capacitor elecrode materials; interface modifications of carbon-based supercapacitor electrodes; and redox deposition of nanoscale MnO2 on ultraporous carbon nanoarchitectures: correlation of MnO2 deposition time and electrochemical performance.
