The proceedings contain 81 papers. The topics discussed include: influence of the management strategies on the sizing of hybrid supply composed with battery and supercapacitor; electrical architecture for high power segmented PEM fuel cell in vehicle application; energy management by coordination control of DC bus voltage in PV hydrogen system; determination of the exploitable solar energy for electricity generation using the photovoltaic systems; towards understanding the effects of climatic and environmental factors on solar PV performance in arid desert regions (Qatar) for various PV technologies; utilization of supercapacitors to reduce lead acid battery stresses in UPS; design of a multifunction low power converter with reduced freedom degrees for photovoltaic and mobile applications; parametric study of a flat plate wick assisted heat pipe solar collector; and a real time implementation of an improved MPPT controller for photovoltaic systems.
