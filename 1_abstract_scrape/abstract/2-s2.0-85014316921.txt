The proceedings contain 76 papers. The topics discussed include: effects of magnesium potassium phosphate cements mixed with silica fume on the solidification and reduction of municipal sludge; preparation and characterization of bio-composite PEEK/Nha; fabrication of micro-nano composite textured surface for slurry sawn mc-Si wafers cell; experimental study on heat transfer performance of aluminum foam parallel-flow condenser in air conditioner; understanding electronic and optical properties of strontium titanate at both PBE and HSE06 levels; investigation of the abnormal Zn diffusion phenomenon in III-V compound semiconductors induced by the surface self-diffusion of matrix atoms; and preparation and characterization of Ni-doped carbon aerogel for supercapacitor.
