The proceedings contain 47 papers. The topics discussed include: a comparative analysis of metaheuristic techniques for improving PTE and pdl of a wireless power transfer system; constructive heuristics and mathematical formulation for solving the permutation flow shop scheduling problem with setup time; optimal design of type 3 and type 4 linear phase fir differentiators using the genetic algorithm; genetic algorithms for optimal control of a continuous model of a diabetic population; modeling of fuel cell by using proteus; an algorithm for gate resizing to reduce power dissipation in combinational digital designs; energy management of battery/supercapacitor electric vehicle considering regenerative braking control; and smart medical devices to help patients and health workers: a survey.
